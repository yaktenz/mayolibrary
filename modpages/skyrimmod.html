What to do if it says "version out of date"?

  1. Delete the four files you pasted earlier after noting down your version number after launching it and getting the prompt message.
  2. Download the archive that matches your version number from the **Version Directory** link above.
  3. Extract the four files into your Skyrim directory: "skse.dll" "Data (Folder)" "skseloader.exe" "steamloader.dll".
  4. Paste them in your directory.
  5. Launch the game. If the message doesn't appear, you've successfully updated it.
